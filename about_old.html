My Story

I started my career as a forced developer. It was the era of outsourced software engineering jobs in India and my parents did their best to steer me towards a stable career.

However, I had always been sketching and painting and was keen to pursue a carreer in art & design.I moved to Sweden to study Industrial Design at Chalmers. Sorry, mom & dad.

In Sweden, my travails took me to Volvo and Lotus, where I did internships in steering wheel design and suspension design. In my final year, I began exploring shapes in 3D using Alias and Rhino. My major was in NURBS surface modeling. My thesis was written at the Mercedes Benz Design Studio in Sindelfingen where i worked on Neues Türöffnungskonzept or New DoorOpening Concept for Mercedes Benz SLS AMG.

After graduation, I started working as a concept artist for a transportation startup in India. I was wearing many hats- from illustrator to Class A modeler. One of India's first concept car was the Scarab and I was one of the lead designers.

My latest gig has been with Altair where I started as an Industrial Design subject matter expert (SME) for a 3D/CAD tool. After working with designers for several years, I realized the impact of user expereince on software design.

In 2021, I helped build and launch Altair Design System -Unity. Today, we have wide spread adoption and are working on V 2.0 of Unity, that utilizies design tokens and variables.
