THEN & NOW

I started my career as a mainframe developer working for williams-sonoma. It was an era of outsourced software engineering jobs in India and my parents did their best to steer me towards a stable career.

However, I had always been sketching and painting and was keen to pursue a career in art and/or design.I moved to Sweden to study Industrial Design at Chalmers.

In Sweden, my travails took me to Volvo and Lotus, where I completed internships in steering wheel design and suspension design. In my final year, I began exploring shapes in 3D using Alias and Rhino. My major was in NURBS surface modeling. My thesis was written at the Mercedes Benz Design Studio in Sindelfingen where I worked on Neues Türöffnungskonzept or New DoorOpening Concept for Mercedes Benz cars.

After graduation, I started working as a concept artist for a transportation startup in India. This was back when there was no venture funding, and we were self-funded, i,e sweat and tears. I was wearing many hats - from designing vehicle interfaces and presenting to clients, to paying electricity bills and running miscellaneous errands.

My latest gig has been with Altair where I started as an Industrial Design subject matter expert (SME) for a 3D/CAD tool.

Over the past decade, I have had the pleasure of working with amazing designers from firms such as UnderArmour, Tervis, Wilson Golf, Dimensions Furniture, to name a few.

In 2021, I helped build and launch Altair Design System -Unity. Today, we have wide spread adoption and are working on V 2.0 of Unity that utilizies design tokens and variables.
