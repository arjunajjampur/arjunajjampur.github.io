Frame and Reframe

I strive to find simple solutions to complex problems. This begins with clear and intentional communication with all stakeholders. Understanding the problem well minimizes rework.

It is akin to measure twice, cut once.

Outcome over Output

My job is not to eliminate all friction and create large number of outputs, but to create clear, intuitive experiences that result in a better outcome for people.

Always ask, So what?

UX is Strategy

As an designer, I find tranquility working in a silo. However, I also want to ensure my work is aligned with broader business goals - top line, EBITDA.

If not, Why bother?
